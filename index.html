RL · Autonomous Vehicles . CS238
Contextual Risk Distortion: Modeling Driver Irrationality via IRL
Integrated Cumulative Prospect Theory with Max Entropy IRL to model context-dependent irrational driving behavior. Trained on 1,798 NGSIM trajectories; achieved 42.72% accuracy — a 10.4% relative improvement over rational baselines, with largest gains in high-stakes driving contexts.
NLP · CS224N
Where Does LoRA Actually Help? Probing Layer-Wise Adaptation in GPT-2
Implemented GPT-2 (small) from scratch and ran a controlled LoRA ablation across two structurally distinct tasks, fixing parameter budgets to isolate adaptation target as the sole variable. Analysis included per-class recall decomposition, epoch-level stability profiling, and length-stratified subset analysis to diagnose why single-module strategies diverge — falsifying the mechanistic hypothesis in both directions.
Robotics · ROS2 · CS237A
Autonomous TurtleBot Navigation Stack
Built and tested a ROS2-based autonomy stack for TurtleBot 3 using Python, Gazebo, RViz, Docker, and hardware deployment. Implemented navigation, perception-triggered control, and frontier exploration, validated in simulation and on the physical robot.

AI Governance · Policy

Data Systems · CS145
Stack Overflow Success Dynamics: Reputation, Prestige, and Trend Adoption
SQL-driven analysis of 50GB+ Stack Overflow data across 6 BigQuery tables, investigating whether users recover from weak first posts, whether high reputation inflates upvotes independent of answer quality, and whether experienced users lead or follow emerging technology trends. Used correlated subqueries, multi-step CTEs, and window functions to surface career-phase engagement patterns and identify technologies in their "golden hour" of rising interest and quality engagement.
